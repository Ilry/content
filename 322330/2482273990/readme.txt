Alter Guardian hat will automatically open when you equip it or enter caves, which is really annoying, this mod relief you from that pain. 

Also, this mod adds some extra options to make the hat even more powerful:
1. The hat is now craftable, so is alter guardian hat shard, needs celestial three tech.
2. Change the value of sanity loss everytime your character attacks (with alterguardian hat equiped, of course), available options: [2, 1, 0, -1, -2] (-1 is the default value in the game). Set this option to 1 or 2, your character can even gain sanity by every attack (like wigfrid), cool !
3. Change the activation threshold of the hat, when your character's sanity falls below this threshold, the hat won't provide light buff, and no more gestalt spawned when your character attacks. Available options: [0%, 30%, 50%, 80%, 85%, 90%] (85% is the default value in the game), set this option to 0% will make the hat stay activated forever.
4. Change the number of gestalt spawned every time your character attacks, available options: [0, 1, 2, 3]. Now you can disable gestalt attack by setting this option to 0.
5. Alter Guardian hat can be used as an indestructible armor, you can decide its absorb percent, available options: [0%, 20% 50%, 80% 90%, 95%, 100%].
6. The hat can provide more damage when attacking others, choose your multiplier, available options: [1, 1.25, 1.5, 2, 4, 8].
7. The hat can provide more walk speed.
8. The hat can be waterproof.
9. You will never be cold or overheated wearing this hat.
10. You are fire immune wearing this hat.
11. This hat provide goggles vision when you walking into storms.
12. Beefalo won't attack during beefalo mating season.

启蒙皇冠在主动装备或者上下洞穴时会自动打开，露出5个格子，每次需要手动关闭，有点烦人。。。。
这个mod可以禁止启蒙皇冠自动打开，需要打开时装备之后右键单击即可。
另外提供了启蒙皇冠的增强选项：
1. 皇冠和碎片现在可以制作了，在天体科技栏制作。
2. 调整人物每次攻击时的掉san值，游戏默认是每攻击一次减1点san值，可以调整为不掉san，甚至像女武神那样打怪升san。
3. 调整激活启蒙皇冠需要的san值，游戏默认当人物拥有85%以上的san值时启蒙皇冠会提供照明并且每次攻击附带召唤一个月岛精灵的效果，帮助玩家攻击。调整为0%会让启蒙皇冠永久处于激活状态。
4. 调整人物每次攻击时召唤的月岛精灵的个数，设置为0则不再召唤月岛精灵。
5. 启蒙皇冠可以提供护甲，无耐久，护甲值可以选择。
6. 皇冠可以提供攻击力加成。
7. 皇冠可以提供速度加成。
8. 拥有防雨效果。
9. 保持舒适的温度，永远不会过冷或者过热。
10. 免疫燃烧伤害。
11. 在风暴中提供护目镜效果。
12. 发情的牛不会主动攻击玩家。
